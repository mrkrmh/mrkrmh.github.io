Standard strong-line diagnostics are often heavily biased by the intense radiation fields of AGNs. This ongoing project focuses on deriving new metallicity calibrations (specifically N2O2 and N2S2) that remain unaffected by X-ray luminosity and electron density variations, paving the way for analyzing high-resolution spectra from next-generation observatories.
